HCMUT Guide

Ho Chi Minh City University of Technology (HCMUT) is one of the most prestigious universities in Vietnam. It is located in Ho Chi Minh City, the largest city in Vietnam. HCMUT has a beautiful campus with many trees, flowers, and modern buildings. There are many facilities and resources available to students, including classrooms, laboratories, libraries, dormitories, sports complexes, and a large student body.

Here are some tips for getting around HCMUT:

  • The best way to get around HCMUT is by walking or biking. The campus is relatively small and easy to get around on foot or by bike.
