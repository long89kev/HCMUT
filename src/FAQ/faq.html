HCMUT FAQ

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about HCMUT:

  • Where is HCMUT located? HCMUT is located in Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam.
  • What is the best way to get around HCMUT? The best way to get around HCMUT is by walking or biking.
  • How can I get to a different part of the city? You can take a bus or taxi to a different part of the city.
  • Does HCMUT have a shuttle bus service? Yes, HCMUT has a shuttle bus service that runs between the main campus and the university's satellite campuses.
